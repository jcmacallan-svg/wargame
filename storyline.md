# Multi-Domain Wargame Scenario (Non-Classified)

## Scenario Overview

A fictional regional crisis escalates between two blocs after rising
geopolitical tension. The exercise focuses on multi-domain coordination
between land, air, maritime, cyber, space and information environments.

## Storyline Arc

Phase 1 -- Competition - Increased reconnaissance activity - Information
campaigns online - Supply chain disruptions

Phase 2 -- Hybrid Pressure - Coordinated cyber intrusions - Maritime
harassment incidents - Border troop movements

Phase 3 -- Limited Conflict - Drone strikes on infrastructure - Air
intercept incidents - Naval blockade attempt

Phase 4 -- Major Escalation - Multi-domain operations - Strategic
command decisions - Civil-military crisis response

## Exercise Goals

-   Improve situational awareness
-   Practice cross-domain coordination
-   Test escalation decision making
